The internet is essentially a larger group of computers sharing data.
The computers share info with eachother over the web, and send a series or requests and responses.
Your computer sends a request to a server, which then sends a response with the data you requested.
This could be a web page, or all sorts of data.

When you click on a link, it sends you to a page specified in the html file. 
The target is set with a URL, or uniform resource locator.
Clicking on the link sends a request to the specified URL, and they send you a response with the appropriate web page.
This could be a link to another site, a link to another page on their site, or even something lieka  download.
